Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate?

You can donate directly by clicking the "Recharge" button and selecting an amount to contribute. Once recharged, you can distribute the funds to the cause of your choice.

Where will my donations go?

Your donations will be used to support ongoing relief efforts for crises such as floods, protests, and other emergencies affecting Bangladesh. You can track the impact of your donations through the "History" section.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

It depends on your country of residence. Please consult your local tax laws or a tax advisor for information on tax-deductible donations.

Can I choose which cause to support?

Yes, after recharging your account, you can choose which specific cause, such as flood relief or protest aid, to support by donating your available balance to that cause.

How can I track my donations?

You can view your donation history, including the amount, cause, and date of each contribution, by visiting the "History" section on our website.
